Afghan Curry Braise

Afghan Curry Braise is an earthy, rustic, and bright blend inspired by the heart of Afghanistan. This unique blend combines the traditional flavors of curry with the floral notes of our signature saffron, offering a culinary experience that's both familiar and novel. Ideal for balancing robust flavors, it pairs beautifully with red meats, white fish, and hearty vegetables. Whether you're crafting a traditional Afghan dish or experimenting with modern fusion, this blend promises to be your go-to spice.
